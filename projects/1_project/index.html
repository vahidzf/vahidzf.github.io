Geriatric Health and Exercise

BioPhychoSocial Approach to Exercise Rehabilitation.

This category focuses on studies related to the aging population, addressing issues such as balance, functional fitness, and pain management. Research within this category explores exercise interventions tailored to older adults, aiming to enhance their mobility, stability, and overall quality of life. A sample of my studies are referenced below.
